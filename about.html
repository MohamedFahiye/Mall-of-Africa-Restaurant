About Our Restaurant

Established in 2020, Mall of Africa Restaurant is the passion project of two lifelong friends and culinary experts who wanted to share the rich, diverse flavors of African cuisine with the world. Our mission is to offer not just food, but an experience of culture, hospitality, and tradition from across the African continent.
